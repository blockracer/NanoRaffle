About

Nano Raffle is a raffle where users put in entries which cost 1 NANO each to build up a pot. Once every 5 days a winner is drawn and wins 90% of the pot. 2.5% goes to the Protocol Community fund and 7.5% goes to the owner.

If you would like to put multiple entries in, simply send a single transaction to the raffle address with the amount of NANO equal to the entries you would like, for example 2, 5 or 10 NANO. Any amount sent that is less than 1 NANO will not give you an entry.
